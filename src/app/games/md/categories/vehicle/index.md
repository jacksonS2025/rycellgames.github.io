## What makes a vehicle game?

There are a lot of things that make a vehicle game, but here's a few factors they need:

**Vehicles:** Yes, of course, a vehicle game must have vehicles! But what sort of vehicles? Well, most vehicle games
consist of vehicles like Motorbikes (like Moto X3M), cars (like drift hunters) or even planes!

**Competition:** Good vehicle games should have some serious **COMPETITION** to create a good experience, and that could be any type of competition,
like a race, or just a leaderboard!

**Physics:** Vehicle games should have logically sound physics that seem realistic so it can mimic **real** environments, like in races!

**Customization:** Many vehicle games provide and offer options to customize their vehicles, like changing the skin to a pattern, painiting, or changing the model of the vehicle.
